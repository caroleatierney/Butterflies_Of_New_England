Inspiration for this Project

Driving my friend, Kristin, to Logan Airport, after seeing Bruce Springsteen my fifth time, I mentioned that I only saw one butterfly this Summer on the side of the highway. I had just heard at the Marshfield Fair that Monarch Butterflies are now endangered. There are not enough Milkweeds along their migration path and Milkweeds are their only source of food. She mentioned that once, on the Vineyard, she saw a ton of Monarchs land and within a few minutes they flew off again.
