Inspiration for this Project

While with my friend, I mentioned that I only saw one butterfly this Summer (2023) on the side of the highway. I had just heard at the Marshfield Fair that Monarch Butterflies are now endangered. There are not enough Milkweeds along their migration path and Milkweeds are their only source of food. She mentioned that once, on Martha's Vineyard, she saw a ton of Monarchs land and within a few minutes they flew off again. While researching Monarch Migration on the Vineyard, I came across this famous Martha's Vineyard video. Click the link to view it.

The butterfly display at the Marshfield Fair exhibit was provided by Butterflies of Cape Cod. They want to bring awareness to Butterfly Conservation. This is a link to their website.
